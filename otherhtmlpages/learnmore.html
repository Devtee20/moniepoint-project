Frequently Asked Questions

What is a debit card?

A debit card is a payment card that allows you to make purchases by deducting funds directly from your linked bank account, often with the option to withdraw cash from ATMs.

How do I request a Moniepoint Debit Card?

Simply download the Moniepoint app to request your debit card. Cards are delivered within 48hrs to your doorstep.

How long does it take to receive my debit card after applying for it?

Within 48hours.

How do I activate my Debit card?

To activate your Moniepoint Debit Card,open the app and activate your card.

Where can I use my Moniepoint Debit Card?

You can use your Moniepoint Debit Card at any merchant, POS or ATM that accepts debit card payments.

How many ATMs accept the Moniepoint Debit Cards?

Moniepoint Debit Cards are accepted at all ATMS nationwide

What do I do if my Moniepoint Debit Card is lost, stolen or misplaced?

If your debit card is lost, stolen, or misplaced, you should immediately disable it through the mobile app and contact Moniepoint's customer service to report it.

What do I do if I forget my pin?

If you forget your PIN for your Moniepoint Debit Card, you can reset it through the mobile app or online banking.

What is the daily or monthly spending limit on my debit card?

There is no daily, weekly or monthly spending limit on your debit card.

Can I use my debit card for online purchases, and is it secured for online transactions?

Yes, you can use your Moniepoint Debit Card for online purchases, and it is often secured for online transactions
