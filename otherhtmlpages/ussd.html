Frequently Asked Questions

Will I be affected as an existing customer?

As an existing customer, you can enable the USSD channel from your mobile or web applications via settings. You will need to authorise this action using your user PIN.

How much would it cost to use USSD?

Dialing the Moniepoint USSD code *5573# doesn't cost anything.

What are the shortcodes and menu codes for all services on USSD?

You can either access the specific flow by dialing the main USSD code *5573# and selecting the relevant flow from the main menu or dial a slightly extended code that can directly lead you to the needed flow. The available shortcodes and menu codes include:

Can I accept transfers with my Moniepoint POS terminal?

The various USSD functions and their limits include: Transfer single limit: 20,000 Transfer daily cumulative limit: 100,000 Airtime single and daily limit: 10,000 Data single and daily limit: 10,000

Will everyone with my phone have access to my private information?

No, any sensitive operation (seeing the balance, performing any kind of transaction, changing the user PIN, etc.) will need to be authorised through the user PIN.

What if I see a message telling me that the channel is not enabled after trying to dial the USSD code?

The channel can be enabled from either the mobile application (business or personal) or the web app. On the mobile apps, click on your profile picture -> Click on settings -> Turn on the USSD Banking feature. On the web app, click the settings icon -> Security tab -> USSD banking toggle. Note that enabling/disabling the channel requires PIN authorisation.

Is the USSD channel free?

To maintain a reliable 24/7 service, we've introduced a ₦10 fee for transactional services like transfers, airtime/data purchases, balance checks, and PIN changes. Essential services like getting an OTP, activating your account, and blocking your account/card via the Emergency Menu remain completely free. We'll keep this list updated as we go. Some services might move between the “billable” and “free” lists in the future, depending on how costs and market conditions change, but you'll always be informed ahead of time.

Can I disable the USSD channel after enabling it?

Yes, you can always enable/disable the channel from the settings pages in the personal & business applications and on the web application.

What if I see a message telling me that I don't have a moniepoint account even though I do?

Make sure you dial the USSD code from your registered mobile number with your account; this could happen when you dial the USSD code from an unregistered mobile number.

What if I don't see all my moniepoint accounts in the list of accounts?

If you have chosen to use a different phone number during your onboarding process from business to personal or vice versa, that will affect the accounts you see when you use the phone number to dial the USSD code. For example: If a user already has a Moniepoint business profile and account using phone number A, logs into the personal app, and chooses a different phone number B for personal use, dialing the USSD code with phone number A will only allow them to see their business accounts, while dialing with phone number B will show only their personal account(s).

Will everyone with my phone have access to my private information?

No, any sensitive operation (seeing the balance, performing any kind of transaction, changing the user PIN, etc.) will need to be authorised through the user PIN.

Can I block my own account(s)?

Yes, you can by dialing the USSD code *5573*911# from your phone number and choosing the "Stop Debit transactions" and then "for yourself" options, which will prompt you to enter your user PIN. If verified successfully, it will block all your Moniepoint accounts (business, personal, and business owner accounts).

How do I block my account if my phone is stolen?

You can now dial the Emergency Menu code *5573*911# from any phone, even one not registered to you. Follow the prompts for "Stop Debit transactions" to instantly secure your account.

What if my phone was stolen, and I don't have access to the SIM?

You can still block your account(s) if someone else has access to the Moniepoint USSD channel. They can dial *5573*911#, choose "Stop Debit transactions" → "for others", then provide your phone number or account number. They will be asked for your user PIN (not theirs). If verified, your accounts will be blocked.

Can I unblock my account(s)?

Yes. You can now unblock your own account from any phone using the *5573*911# menu, as long as you provide the correct phone number or account number and PIN.

Can I open a new Moniepoint account using USSD?

Not at the moment, but this feature will be available soon.

What is the difference between "Change PIN" (*5573*6#) and "Forgot PIN" (*5573*7#)?

Change User PIN (*5573*6#) is used when you know your current PIN and want to change it. Forgot User PIN (*5573*7#) is used when you cannot remember your PIN and need to reset it.
